Take Action

  1. Check your records

    Make sure you and your family are up to date on all recommended vaccines. If you’re unsure, ask your healthcare provider.

  2. Spread facts, not fear

    Share accurate vaccine information from reliable sources like the CDC, WHO, or your local health department.

  3. Support public health efforts

    • Encourage schools and workplaces to offer vaccine education
    • Advocate for accessible vaccination clinics
    • Stand against misinformation in your community

  4. Talk to your doctor

    If you have concerns about vaccines, ask questions. Healthcare providers can give clear, personalized answers based on science—not fear.
