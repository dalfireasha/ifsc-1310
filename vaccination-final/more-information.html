More Information & Resources:

Staying informed with reliable, science-backed information is one of the best ways to protect yourself, your family, and your community. Below are some trustworthy organizations and resources where you can learn more about vaccines, current outbreaks, and how to take action.

Trusted Health Organizations:

Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)

Up-to-date vaccine schedules, outbreak alerts, and educational materials.

World Health Organization (WHO)

Global vaccine data, Q&A, and myth-busting information.

Vaccinate Your Family

A nonprofit organization offering easy-to-understand vaccine information and advocacy tools.

American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP)

Vaccine guidance for children, teens, and families.
